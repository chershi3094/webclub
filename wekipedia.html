WONDERS OF THE WORLD

There are seven wonders in the world:

  • Taj Mahal,India
  • Christ the Redeemer,Brazil
  • Great Wall of China,China
  • Petra,Jordan
  • Machu Pichu,Peru
  • Chichen Itza,Mexico
  • Colosseum,Italy

1.Taj Mahal

Built in agra by mughal emperror Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the mausoleum of white marbel is a marvel to behold.

2.Christ the Redeemer

Located atop the Corcovado Mountain in Tijuca Forest National Park,statue of Christ the Redeemer is perched at a height of 700 meterrs.It is one of the i conic landmarks of Brazil and Rio de Janeiro.

3.Great Wall of China

The Great Wall was continuously built from the 3rd century BC to the 17th century AD on the northern border of the country as the miltry defence project of successive Chinese Empires,with a total length of more than 20,000 kilometers.

4.Petra

Located in the southwestern desert of jordan,Petra is the world-famous archaeological site that once served as the Nabatean kingdom's capital and where ancient Eastern traditionals blend with hellenistic architecture.

5.Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu in peru stands 2,430m above sea-level and dates back to the 15th century. It is on the most renowned structure of the Inca civilization.

6.Chichen Itza

The renowned and sacred site of Mayan ruins,the city of Chichen Itza boasts of a world Heritage Site of a massive temple-El Castillo,built in the shape of a pyramid.

7.Colosseum

Constructed mainly of sand and concrete, Colosseum in Italian capital Rome is the largest surviving amphitheature in the world.
